# Cross Country

We want to builds apps that are rich with personal metrics, encourage self reflection, and keep our users coming back so that they value the product enough to pay for it! 

It should be effortless to collect, curate, and create content as well as track its effectiveness across other social platforms and schedule releases. With the right components, we can quickly build a UI to cultivate a community around our content and chart a course which can generate revenue. There also is value in releasing a product that's open source and free to use that can ultimately promote us as invaluable creators.

This library is a collection of components that are geared towards building a community around content curation and plotting courses. There are mix of components well-suited for building a website, media gallery, course, or tile-based game. There's great potential in discovering a new product by combining elements of all of them!
